- **Price range (apartments):** $112,000 - $270,000
- **Price range (villas):** $150,000 - $740,000
- **Price range (townhouses):** $185,000 - $330,000
- **Typical leasehold:** 25-35 years + extension option
- **Projected ROI (developer claims):** 9.5-20% annually
