# Bali Real Estate Market Overview

## Tourism & Economy (2025-2026)

- **Tourist arrivals 2025:** 5.47 million (+251% growth trajectory)
- **Indonesia target 2026:** 14-17 million international tourists
- **Bali GDP growth:** ~10% regional
- **Key driver:** Post-pandemic recovery, digital nomad influx, emerging as alternative to Dubai

## Market Dynamics

### Demand Drivers
- Russian-speaking investors (primary audience for most developers)
- European buyers (growing, especially for premium/wellness concepts)
- Digital nomads driving rental demand (especially Canggu, Ubud)
- Infrastructure development (new roads, airport expansion plans)

### Supply Landscape
- **Boom in new development:** dozens of new complexes announced 2024-2026
- **Shift toward managed properties:** hotel operators (Wyndham, etc.) increasingly attached to projects
- **Nuanu Creative City:** major new development hub (~44 ha), attracting multiple developers
- **Popular development areas:** Canggu (saturating), Uluwatu/Bukit (growing), Nusa Dua (established), Ubud (niche)

### Risks & Concerns
- **Oversupply risk** in popular areas (Canggu, Seminyak)
- **Airbnb regulation tightening** (see [regulations.md](regulations.md))
- **Leasehold uncertainty** - extension not legally guaranteed
- **Construction quality variance** - ranges from ISO-certified to informal
- **Geopolitical factors** - Middle East tensions affecting tourism flows
- **Currency risk** - IDR fluctuations vs USD pricing

## Market Segments

### Investment Properties (Hotel-managed)
- Apartments/suites with guaranteed management (Wyndham, OXO, etc.)
- Entry: $112K-$260K
- Target ROI: 9.5-20% (developer projections)
- Typical: 30-90m2, leasehold 25-35 years

### Lifestyle Villas
- Standalone or complex villas for personal use + rental income
- Entry: $150K-$700K+
- Typical: 100-300m2 building, 100-600m2 land
- Management via agencies or self-managed

### Land Plots
- Raw land for development
- Prices: 7-11M IDR per are/year (leasehold), varies hugely by location
- Requires PBG (building permit), zoning check

### Townhouses / Co-living
- Growing segment, targets digital nomads and small investors
- Entry: $185K-$330K
- Typical: 70-140m2, private pool, shared infrastructure

## Key Metrics for Marketing

When creating marketing content, reference these data points:
- "5.47 million tourists visited Bali in 2025"
- "ROI 9.5-20% annually on managed properties"
- "Entry from $112,000 for hotel-managed apartments"
- "Leasehold 25-35 years with extension options"
- "Bali GDP growing at ~10%"
- "250m from beach" (for Surfside), "80m from beach" (OceaniQ) - proximity matters
