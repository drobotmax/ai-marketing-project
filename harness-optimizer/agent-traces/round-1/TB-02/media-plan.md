# Media Plan: Serenity Villas, Ubud

## Общие параметры

- **Бюджет:** $5,000/мес
- **Гео:** International (primary: RU/CIS speakers worldwide, secondary: EN speakers interested in Bali)
- **Период:** Month 1-3 (launch + optimization)
- **Цель:** 100+ лидов/мес при CPL < $50

## Approved Channel Plans

### Channel 1: Meta Ads

- **Owner:** Targeting specialist
- **Objective:** Lead generation (instant forms + site traffic)
- **Approved budget:** $2,500/мес ($2,100 cold + $400 retargeting)
- **Why now:** Visual format идеален для Bali lifestyle creatives. Instant forms снижают friction. Housing category limits targeting, но broad + creative-driven approach работает.
- **Key dependencies:**
  - Meta Pixel + CAPI на serenity-bali.com (BLOCKER)
  - Instant form templates (3 варианта)
  - Creative assets: минимум 6 вариантов (3 static + 3 video/carousel)
  - CRM для lead routing и quality tracking
- **Success signal for Phase 2:** CPL < $40, qualified lead rate > 25%, 60+ leads/month
- **Included specialist components:**
  - Campaign 1: Cold Investor ($45/day)
  - Campaign 2: Cold Lifestyle/Nomad ($25/day)
  - Campaign 3: Retargeting ($13/day)
  - 4 experiments (T1-T4)
- **Deferred / cut items:**
  - LAL audiences (Phase 2, после накопления CRM data)
  - EN language ads on Meta (Phase 2)

### Channel 2: Google Ads

- **Owner:** Contextologist
- **Objective:** Demand capture (search intent) + full funnel (PMax)
- **Approved budget:** $2,010/мес ($1,290 search + $510 PMax + $210 remarketing)
- **Why now:** Google Search ловит горячий интент ("купить виллу бали"). PMax расширяет reach по всем Google surfaces. International market = Google primary (не Яндекс).
- **Key dependencies:**
  - GA4 + GTM setup (BLOCKER)
  - Google Ads conversion tracking (BLOCKER)
  - Landing page speed + mobile optimization audit
  - Creative assets для PMax: images, videos, logos
- **Success signal for Phase 2:** CPL < $60, 35+ leads/month, qualified rate > 20%
- **Included specialist components:**
  - Brand campaign ($3/day)
  - Non-Brand Hot RU ($20/day)
  - Non-Brand Hot EN ($15/day)
  - Competitors ($5/day)
  - PMax ($17/day)
  - Display Remarketing ($7/day)
  - 4 experiments
- **Deferred / cut items:**
  - Broad match keywords (Phase 2, после Smart Bidding)
  - YouTube ads (Phase 3)
  - Google Demand Gen (Phase 3)

## Cross-Channel Optimization Rules

- Если Meta CPL < $30 и Google CPL > $70 на Day 14 - перераспределить $200-300/мес из Google в Meta (в пределах caps)
- Если оба канала дают лиды, но sales не конвертирует - не scale, а audit sales process (speed-to-lead, script, follow-up)
- Если PMax каннибализирует Brand search (check Auction Insights) - добавить brand exclusion в PMax
- Deduplicate leads между каналами: единый CRM, UTM-разметка, attribution window 7 дней
- Weekly cross-channel review: CPL, qualified rate, cost per qualified lead по каждому каналу
