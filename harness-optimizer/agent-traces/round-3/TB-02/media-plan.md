## Conflict Resolution

| Конфликт | Что предложили агенты | Решение planner | Почему |
|----------|----------------------|-----------------|--------|
| Meta vs Google lead attribution | Both may claim same lead if user sees Meta ad then searches Google | Primary attribution to first touchpoint; use UTM consistently across both | Avoid double-counting in CRM |
| WhatsApp leads split | Targeting specialist wants WhatsApp objective on Meta; contextologist doesn't mention | WhatsApp leads exclusively via Meta Ads | Google Search better suited for form-based leads; WhatsApp via Meta aligns with platform strengths |
| Remarketing overlap | Both Meta (IG engagers, site visitors) and Google Display (site visitors) retarget | Deduplicate: Meta handles IG engagers + lead form non-submits; Google Display handles site visitors without IG activity | Reduce frequency overlap |

## Approved Channel Plans

### Channel 1: Meta Ads
- **Owner:** Targeting specialist
- **Objective:** Lead generation (instant form + WhatsApp)
- **Approved budget:** $2,750/month
- **Why now:** Primary demand creation channel; immediate reach to target segments
- **Key dependencies:** Pixel verified on serenity-bali.com, instant form created, 9+ creative variants ready
- **Success signal for Phase 2:** CPL < $40 by end of week 2, lead response rate > 30%
- **Included specialist components:**
  - 3 cold acquisition ad sets (Broad, RU Investors, Digital Nomads)
  - Retargeting campaign (IG engagers, site visitors, form non-submits)
  - WhatsApp lead campaign (Phase 2, day 15+)
- **Deferred / cut items:**
  - CRM LAL audiences (deferred to Phase 2, after 100+ leads collected)

### Channel 2: Google Ads
- **Owner:** Contextologist
- **Objective:** Demand capture (search) + remarketing (display)
- **Approved budget:** $1,750/month
- **Why now:** Captures high-intent users already searching for Bali property
- **Key dependencies:** Google Tag + GA4 on serenity-bali.com, conversion events configured
- **Success signal for Phase 2:** CPL < $60, CTR > 3% on search
- **Included specialist components:**
  - Search EN campaign (4 ad groups: Buy Villa, Investment, Management, Competitor)
  - Search RU campaign (3 ad groups: Купить, Инвестиции, Управление)
  - Display remarketing (site visitors, engaged users)
  - Display cold prospecting (in-market + custom audiences)
- **Deferred / cut items:**
  - Performance Max (deferred to Month 2+, needs more conversion data)
  - YouTube ads (budget insufficient for video reach)

### Channel 3: Instagram Organic
- **Owner:** Copywriter / SMM
- **Objective:** Trust building, social proof, retargeting audience seed
- **Approved budget:** $500/month (content production)
- **Why now:** High-ticket purchase requires trust; organic content supports paid campaigns
- **Key dependencies:** Access to renders, lifestyle photos, project updates from PT Serenity Bali
- **Success signal:** 500+ followers month 1, engagement rate > 3%

## Cross-Channel Optimization Rules

- If Meta CPL drops below $30 while Google CPL stays above $70: consider shifting 20% from Google Display to Meta retargeting
- If Google Search delivers higher quality leads (viewing rate > 25%): protect search budget, cut display cold
- If both channels cannibalize same intent: check UTM data, assign leads by first touchpoint
- If sales team can't handle 100+ leads/month: throttle cold campaigns before scaling
- Weekly cross-channel review: compare CPL, lead quality, response rate across Meta and Google
