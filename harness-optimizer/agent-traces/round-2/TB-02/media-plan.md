## Cross-Channel Optimization Rules

- If Meta CPL drops below $30 and quality holds, consider shifting 10-15% from Google to Meta
- If Google Search impression share hits 90%+ on core keywords, don't increase budget - expand semantics instead
- If both channels produce qualified leads at similar CPCQL, maintain split; if one channel is 2x+ more expensive per qualified lead, shift budget
- If sales team can't handle volume (>5 leads/day uncontacted), pause scaling and fix sales process
- Never scale paid ads without CRM feedback on lead quality - cheap CPL without qualification is waste
- If retargeting frequency exceeds 5/day on any channel, rotate creatives immediately
