## Funnel Benchmarks (Real Estate)

| Stage | Typical conversion rate |
|-------|----------------------|
| Click to lead | 1-5% |
| Lead to qualified | 30-50% |
| Qualified to showing | 20-40% |
| Showing to deal | 15-30% |
| Overall click to deal | 0.1-0.5% |

## Budget Allocation (Typical for Real Estate)

| Channel | Recommended share | Notes |
|---------|------------------|-------|
| Yandex Search | 40-60% | Intent-based, highest quality leads |
| Yandex RSYA | 10-20% | Retargeting and awareness |
| Meta/Instagram | 15-25% | Visual, good for new construction |
| VK Ads | 5-15% | Lead forms, good CPL |
| Google Ads | 0-10% | Limited reach in Russia |

## Notes

- Benchmarks are for Moscow/SPb markets. Regional markets typically have 20-40% lower CPC/CPL
- New construction (primary market) has higher CPL but better conversion to deal
- Secondary market has lower CPL but lower deal conversion
- Luxury segment (>15M RUB) has 2-3x higher CPL than mass market
- These benchmarks are based on 2025-2026 market data and Dima's operational experience
- Update quarterly as market conditions change
